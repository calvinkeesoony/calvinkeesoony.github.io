Who am I?

Hi, I am a freelance web designer at Alpha Bravo Studios and I'm looking to start a career in marketing. I have worked on a couple of websites for musicians as well as a personal project for my dissertation. What I have gained from these experiences are skills in Wordpress, Branding and Communication. I have also been improving my HTML/CSS skills by updating my personal project as much as I can.


Why a career in marketing?

I am fascinated with the digital landscape and how far marketing has come. I want to be able to utilizes different digital marketing methods such as: social media marketing, paid ads, analytics, CRM and so on. I also want to find out how each one is effective. My first introduction to marketing was through Google Analytics, being from a web design background this seemed like a good start. I wanted to know where and how my portfolio was gaining traffic.
