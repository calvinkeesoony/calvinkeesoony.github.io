Bio

POPCAAN

2018-Present

Saint Thomas Parish, Jamaica

Baka Not Nice, aka Baka, is the rap alias of Toronto native and Drake associate Travis Savoury. Prior to making his solo debut, Savoury was part of Drake's security detail and appeared on his employer's releases, including Nothing Was the Same ("From Time"), Views ("Hype"), and More Life ("Free Smoke," "Gyalchester"). Shortly after his deal with Drake's Warner-distributed OVO Sound label was publicized in June 2017, Savoury released the boastful single "Live Up to My Name."
