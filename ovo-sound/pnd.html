Bio

PARTYNEXTDOOR

2013-Present

Mississauga, Canada

PARTYNEXTDOOR is a 24-year old producer, songwriter and singer from Mississauga, Ontario signed to OVO Sound. He released his self-titled, critically-acclaimed mixtape in July of 2013 led by the singles “Over Here” and “Break From Toronto” on iTunes. PARTYNEXTDOOR contributed to “Own It” and “Come Through” on Drake’s Nothing Was the Same album and would join him, Future and Miguel on the “Would You Like A Tour?” concert that fall. PARTYNEXTDOOR released his follow-up album PARTYNEXTDOOR TWO on July 29, 2014 led by the Drake-assisted single “Recognize.”
