October's Very Own ~ EST 2008

OVO is founded by Aubrey "Drake" Graham, Noah “40” Shebib and Oliver El-Khatib.

This was after comeback season and served as Drake's main brand and independant label back in 2008. The brand started to release clothing around 2010 and still operates as a clothing brand to this day with stores in Canada, US and UK, while the music side evolved into OVO Sound.

OVO Sound ~ EST 2012

OVO SOUND is a record label founded by Aubrey "Drake" Graham and long-time friends and partners Noah “40” Shebib and Oliver El-Khatib. The label is based in Toronto, Ontario.

DreamCrew ~ EST 2019

A co-venture between Aubrey "Drake" Graham and Adel "Future" Nur.

DreamCrew currently functions as both a management company and entertainment group; Producing Film, Television and special Projects as well as overseeing Drake's professional career and business portfolio.
