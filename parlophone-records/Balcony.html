Balcony

Balcony is Steven Lester (he also uses Stephen or Steve as a first name in album credits). Lester is a native of Birmingham, England, but came up with the idea for Balcony after moving to Los Angeles. Much like Chris Connelly, Lester's vocal often sounds like Scott Walker by way of David Bowie.

Label: Parlophone Records
